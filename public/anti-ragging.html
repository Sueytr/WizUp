Report an Incident

If you have experienced or witnessed any form of ragging, please report it immediately. All reports are confidential.

What Constitutes Ragging?

  • Any form of physical or mental abuse
  • Verbal abuse, intimidation, or humiliation
  • Economic abuse or financial exploitation
  • Forced participation in unwanted activities
  • Creating a hostile or intimidating environment
  • Any act that violates dignity and self-respect
